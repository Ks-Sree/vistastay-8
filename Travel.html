A Bit About Us

Founded in 2015, Unforgettable Travel Company emerged from a shared vision craft ultra-personalized journeys that resonate deeply and endure long after the return home. We design bespoke journeys which live up to our slogan – Why do Ordinary.

Headquartered in London, with offices in New York, Athens, Split, Melbourne, and Cape Town, we serve discerning clientele who are seeking bespoke luxury travel experiences. We’re honored to be ranked the world’s 5th-best tour operator in Travel + Leisure’s 2025 “World’s Best Awards,” a recognition based on votes from approximately 180,000 travelers. We’re also on the Spears500 list and a Conde Nast Top Travel Specialist, representing the world’s most elite travel brands.

Bespoke Itineraries in Over 50 Destinations

Every journey begins with a conversation. Our in-house destination specialists, each a seasoned explorer with deep local knowledge—design trips tailored precisely to your tastes and preferences. From glacier expeditions in Norway to luxury yacht cruises along the Dalmatian coast to cultural immersion in Kyoto.

Guaranteed Peace of Mind

As a UK registered travel company, all clients who travel with us, country of origin benefit from our financial trip protection. We are a licensed travel company and hold an Air Travel Organisers License (ATOL 7583) issued by the Civil Aviation Authority.

We are also a fully bonded travel company protected by the Association of Bonded Travel Organisers Trust (ABTOT 5381). ABTOT provide financial protection for all UK and non-UK travelers. In the unlikely event we were to cease operating, you will be refunded in full or if you are abroad at the time, repatriated back to your country of origin.

A Passionate Team with a Personal Touch

What truly sets Unforgettable Travel apart is our people. We’ve built a culture founded on care, creativity, and attention to detail. Our team of destination specialists, aren’t just experts in luxury travel, they’re passionate storytellers who have lived and actively travel to the destinations they cover. Whether it’s a family celebrating a milestone or a couple planning the trip of a lifetime, we approach each journey with warmth, and a deep understanding of what makes travel meaningful.
